About Chain Reflex

Chain Reflex is an innovative twist on the traditional Rock Paper Scissors game, built on the Ethereum blockchain. Players compete using MemeCoin, a unique ERC-20 token. The game is designed to offer a fun and decentralized gaming experience, where every match is fair and transparent. With an easy-to-use interface, players can easily join games, make moves, and win rewards. Play now to see if you have what it takes to win!
